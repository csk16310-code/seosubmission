Refund Policy

Last Updated: 20-10-2025

Thank you for choosing SEOSubmission.io. We provide digital SEO submission and related services. Since our work involves digital delivery, manual effort, and immediate processing, this Refund Policy outlines how refunds are handled.

1. No Refunds for Digital Services

All services provided by SEOSubmission.io are non-refundable. This includes but is not limited to:

  • SEO Submission Packages
  • Manual Directory Submissions
  • Social Bookmarking Submissions
  • SEO Reports
  • Any consulting or digital delivery services

Once an order is placed and work has begun, we cannot issue a refund because our service is digital and requires manual time investment.

2. Order Cancellation

Orders cannot be canceled once the submission process or analysis has started. If you need to cancel immediately after purchase, you must contact us within **10 minutes** before work begins.

3. Incorrect or Duplicate Payments

We will refund payments only in cases of:

  • Duplicate charges
  • Accidental double payments
  • Technical issues causing incorrect billing

Refunds in these cases will be processed within **5–7 business days**.

4. Service Delivery Issues

If you have not received the service due to a technical issue, please contact us. We will either:

  • Re-deliver the service
  • Manually process your request

Refunds are not applicable for delay or non-receipt caused by incorrect information provided by the customer (wrong email, wrong website URL, etc.).

5. Quality Concerns

If you are unhappy with the quality of our service, we encourage you to contact us. We will review your concerns and may offer:

  • Clarifications
  • Revisions (if applicable)
  • Additional submissions or support

Refunds will not be issued for dissatisfaction once the service is delivered.
